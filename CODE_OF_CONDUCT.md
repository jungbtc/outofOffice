# Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in outofOffice a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Be respectful, accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best for users and the community, and show empathy. Harassment, sexualized attention, trolling, insults, threats, doxxing, and publishing private information are unacceptable.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban behavior they judge inappropriate. Report incidents privately to the security/contact address published by the project organization. Maintainers will respect the reporter’s privacy and respond proportionately.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
